# Design Prompt — 404 Not Found Page

> Paste `_context.md` di atas prompt ini sebelum dikirim ke Claude.

---

## Goal
Halaman 404 yang on-brand, tidak mengecewakan user, dan membantu mereka menemukan halaman yang tepat.

## Page Route
`src/app/[locale]/not-found.tsx`

## Layout Structure

### Full-page Center Layout
- Background: `bg-base` full viewport
- Red ambient glow subtle di tengah

### Content (centered)
- Large "404" text: text-9xl font-bold text-primary-600/20 (huge, muted)
- Di atas "404": small badge "Page Not Found"
- Title: "Oops! This page doesn't exist" (h1, text-3xl)
- Subtitle: "The page you're looking for might have been moved or deleted."
- 2 buttons: "Go to Homepage" (primary) + "Contact Us" (outline)

### Quick Links Section
- "You might be looking for:"
- 4-6 link cards dalam 2×2 grid:
  - Features → /features
  - Pricing → /pricing
  - Blog → /blog
  - Contact → /contact
- Card kecil: icon + label

## Output
`not-found.tsx` — single file, tidak perlu komponen terpisah
